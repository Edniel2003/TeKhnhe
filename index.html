About Us

Group 3 of research wants to give the privilege of 11th and 12th grade programming a website that they can access that has relevancy. This website is designed to provide resources, tools, and information that support and enhance their learning experience. A relevant website for Group 3 programming students is to be designed to complement and support their classroom learning experience, providing them with the resources and tools they need to excel in their studies and prepare for further studies in computer science or a career in the programming field. One way to ensure that the website remains up-to-date is by incorporating open-source libraries, which are constantly evolving and improving. This would allow the website to integrate the latest developments in technology and programming into its resources and tools. Another way to maintain the relevance of the website is by encouraging community contributions and feedback. Since the website is based on an open-source library, it can be modified and improved by anyone with the necessary expertise. By allowing users to contribute to the website's content, the website can remain current and relevant to the needs of its users.

Vision & Mission

Mission

"We are committed to providing Grade 12 programming students with access to relevant and up-to-date resources and tools through the use of open-source libraries. Our dedication to open-source libraries stems from our belief in the power of collaboration and innovation, and our desire to create a more accessible and inclusive learning environment. We are dedicated to incorporating open-source libraries into our curriculum and teaching practices to enhance student learning and prepare them for a future in which open-source technologies play an increasingly important role. Our commitment to open-source libraries includes not only utilizing these libraries in our teaching but also actively contributing to their development and improvement. We believe that open-source libraries have the potential to transform the field of computer science, making it more democratic, inclusive, and innovative. By dedicating ourselves to this mission, we strive to create a future in which Grade 12 programming students have access to the best possible learning resources and are prepared to make a positive impact in the tech industry."

Vision

"Our vision is to empower Grade 12 programming students with the skills and knowledge they need to be successful in the tech industry through the use of tekhne website. We believe that by integrating tekhne website into our curriculum, we can create a learning environment that fosters innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity. We envision a future in which Grade 12 programming students are equipped with the latest tools and technologies, including tekhne website, to tackle real-world problems and create positive change in the world. We believe that by teaching students how to use and contribute to tekhne website, we can create a generation of developers who are not only skilled but also committed to making a positive impact on society. We envision a world in which tekne website are the norm rather than the exception, and where developers collaborate across borders, cultures, and backgrounds to create innovative solutions that benefit everyone. By promoting the use of tekhne website in our Grade 12 programming curriculum, we aim to contribute to this vision and help create a more inclusive and equitable tech industry."
